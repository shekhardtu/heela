## What is Hee?

Hee is a shared edge service for SaaS products that need to offer **custom domains** to their customers. Your customer adds a single CNAME record; Hee handles TLS, routing, and the entire operational burden so your application stays focused on its actual job.

## Why another SaaS edge?

The existing options are all wrong in different ways:

- **Cloudflare for SaaS** — the features you need (Custom Origin SNI, predictable Worker routing) are Enterprise-only. Starts at five figures.
- **Approximated** — closed source, per-domain pricing that scales poorly past ~200 customers.
- **Rolling your own** — totally viable, but you'll spend a month on Caddy config, certificate rate limits, and a multi-tenant API.

Hee is the shared middle ground: **open source** so you can self-host if you grow out of SaaS pricing, **priced for real SaaS teams** (€19/mo for 25 domains), and **built on standards** (Let's Encrypt, Caddy, Postgres — nothing exotic).
